Who we are ?
We are technology savvy and environment friendly team working to build a platform to unite everyone on this planet - we literally mean “every one ” to become aware of the climate change and global warming and what actions each and everyone can take at the comfort of their mobile phone to contribute for greater greener future of this planet and leave a better place for our kids.
About UTG

Its a platform created with a goal to not make money but to save our mother earth from damages done to it by humanity. Human desire to expand their comfort and luxury have impacted this planet in macro levels and as scientists point out we are almost near point of no return.

This platform is created as mobile app so everyone can access it immediately, learn about the damages we are doing to our planet, Start contributing to save planet from day one. There are four areas where anyone can contribute - Energy, Water, Plastics, and Flora & Fauna.

Our platform brings not just awareness but we are going to invest the money we are making in this platform to research innovative products and processes that would help our Mother Nature to slow down and reverse from the negative impacts it had for decades.
